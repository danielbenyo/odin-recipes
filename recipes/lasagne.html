Lasagne

Description

So basically, what you have to do is grease your pan with some oil and stack everything layer by layer

A layer of pasta followed by a layer of sauce and bechamel, and so on

Ingredients

  • Premade, boxed lasagne pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Lasagne premix
  • Lots and lots of cheese
  1. Start by preheating the oven to 220 Celsius
  2. Start cooking your ground beef on some olive il
  3. After the meat is cooked all the way through, add the lasagne premix
  4. Oil the pan and add the first layer of boxed pasta
  5. Lay down the lasagne seasoned ground beef
  6. Rinse and repeat until you run out of ingrediends
  7. Top it off with a generous amount of grated cheese
  8. Put in the preheated oven for 40 minutes or until golden brown top
