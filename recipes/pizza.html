Pizza

Description

This is the most wonderful pizza you will ever have

With pretty much ZERO EFFORT, apart from going to the store and buying it

Ingredients

  • Store bought pizza
  • Lots and lots of love
  1. Start by preheating your oven to 220 Celsius
  2. Take your store bought frozen pizza out of the freezer
  3. Carefully unwrap it from the misterious plastic wrap, make sure not to lose any of the delicious toppings
  4. If you have a pizza stone for the oven use that
  5. If you don't, use tinfoil in your oven rack!
  6. Carefully place the frozen pizza on oven rack
  7. Bake until the crust is golden brown
